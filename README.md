## What OBSIDIAN Is

OBSIDIAN is not a trading bot. It is not a signal feed. It is not a dashboard with charts.

OBSIDIAN is an autonomous capital intelligence system вҖ” a governed, multi-agent deliberation architecture that ingests real-time market sensor data, fuses it across a structured ontology, debates it through an eight-agent adversarial council, enforces it against a constitutional law layer, and delivers sourced, annotated, fully structured investment cases to a single operator.

Where most systems chase signals, OBSIDIAN builds conviction.

It operates with the posture of a senior portfolio manager and the precision of a weapons system. **Silent until certain. Lethal when it acts.**

## The Glass Room

Every thesis that reaches conviction has survived eight independent analytical lenses вҖ” a formal deliberation process where each agent produces a structured verdict object, not a sentiment, not a score, not a string. Visible from the inside. Opaque from the outside.

| Agent | Mandate | Kill Authority |
|---|---|---|
| **ORACLE** | Regime classification. Runs first. Conditions all downstream logic. | вҖ” |
| **VECTOR** | Institutional flow intelligence. Translates sensor data into positioning theses. | вҖ” |
| **AXIOM** | Statistical edge. Win rate, expectancy, R-multiple viability. No edge, no trade. | вҖ” |
| **REFLEX** | Second-order effects. Soros-style feedback loop detection and exhaustion signals. | вҖ” |
| **SPECTER** | Adversarial intelligence. Sole mandate: find the reason this trade is wrong. | 1.5Г— weight |

| **WARDEN** | Constitutional officer. Enforces the Five Laws. Issues REDLINEs. | **Absolute** |

**SPECTER + PHANTOM consensus** triggers a mandatory second deliberation вҖ” the first round is discarded, all agents re-vote with the adversarial case explicitly surfaced. Two adversarial agents in agreement is a hard stop.

**WARDEN** holds absolute kill authority. A REDLINE terminates deliberation immediately. No appeal. No override. The constitutional check is structural вҖ” not advisory.

LAW 1   CAPITAL PROTECTION
        No position > 5% of portfolio. No sector > 20%.
        8% portfolio drawdown: all new positions halted until operator resets.

LAW 2   EVIDENCE BEFORE CONVICTION
        No Decision Package without a conviction score from full deliberation.
        Minimum 6 of 8 agents must render verdicts. Below 6: no package.

LAW 3   REGIME ALIGNMENT
        No long-biased signal in a confirmed bear regime.
        No short-biased signal in a confirmed bull regime.

LAW 4   DEFINED RISK ON EVERY TRADE
        Every authorized trade has a stop loss and R-multiple before routing.
        Minimum R-multiple: 1.5x. Below floor: WARDEN blocks.

LAW 5   HUMAN COMMAND AUTHORITY
        OBSIDIAN does not execute autonomously. Every trade requires
        a Decision Package, operator authorization, and OBSIDIAN routing
        the authorized order. The human is always in the loop.

## Design Principles

**Ontology-First** вҖ” Raw sensor data is input. Ontology state is output. No raw data reaches the operator. Every signal passes through the ontology engine before it becomes a claim.

**Conviction-Gated** вҖ” Nothing reaches the operator without a conviction score earned through the full deliberation process. 3вҖ“5 Decision Packages per trading day is the ceiling, not the target.

**Provenance-Complete** вҖ” Every claim traces to an evidence source. Every source traces to an ingestion event. A package with an unsourced claim is rejected before delivery.

**Regime-Conditioned** вҖ” All conviction thresholds, position sizing, and council vote weights are conditioned on the current regime classification. A component that ignores regime is operating without context.

**Constitutional-First** вҖ” WARDEN evaluates every thesis before deliberation begins and again before the Decision Package is assembled. The constitutional check is not a final gate. It is a continuous constraint.

**Silence is Valid** вҖ” OBSIDIAN generating no Decision Package on a given day is correct behavior when no thesis earns conviction. Silence is not a bug. It is epistemological discipline.
